ABOUT

Caroline King is a filmmaker, photographer, and programmer based in New York City. With a keen eye for storytelling and a deep appreciation for independent cinema, she works at the intersection of visual art and curation.

She has programmed for the Atlanta Film Festival, evaluating films with a meticulous and discerning perspective. Her work—spanning directing, screenwriting, and photography—captures striking, authentic moments.

Caroline studied Film and Media Studies at Middlebury College, graduating cum laude with a feature-length screenplay as her capstone.
